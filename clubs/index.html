The Problem We Remove

WHY AFRICAN RECRUITMENT FAILS CLUBS

Most clubs outside the top leagues face the same three walls when they look at African talent. GUNDUA was built against each one.

Unverifiable Information

Ages, identities, and playing histories arrive through informal channels that no recruitment department can stand behind. Age discrepancies alone quietly kill transfers of African players every window.

The Cost of Coverage

A credible scouting presence across even one African region costs more than most recruitment budgets allow. The clubs that profit most from African talent are the ones that can least afford to scout it properly.

Untrusted Intermediaries

The space between African talent and European clubs is crowded with unlicensed operators and pay-to-play schemes. Every unreliable contact costs your department time and exposes your club's name.

Where We Stand

THE LINES WE DO NOT CROSS

Athletes never pay. Registration and discovery are free for players, permanently. A platform funded by desperate families is not one your club should be seen near, and it is not what we are.

Minors are a development pipeline, not inventory. No activity that conflicts with FIFA Article 19. Players under 18 are supported with education and visibility until they can move correctly.

We are a platform, not an agency. GUNDUA does not represent players. Where representation is needed, it is executed by licensed agents under written agreements, keeping every deal clean for your club.
