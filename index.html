The Process

HOW DISCOVERY
WORKS

01
Athlete Registers
Any athlete aged 13-30 across Africa submits their profile — sport, position, location, age, and a clip. No cost, no gatekeeping. The platform belongs to them.
02
Profile Enters the Network
The profile is live in our searchable database, visible to scouts, clubs, academies, and agents across our global partner network. One submission, global reach.
03
Scouts & Clubs Discover
Verified scouts and clubs search by position, region, age, sport. They filter. They find. They connect — through us, with full trail and accountability.
04
Talent Moves
Trial invitations, academy placements, contract pathways — facilitated through the platform with certified agents and verified international partners. This is what discovery becomes.
