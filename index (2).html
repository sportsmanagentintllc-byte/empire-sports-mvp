The Talent Supply Is Unmatched

Africa supplies a disproportionate share of Europe's top professional footballers. Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire and Cameroon alone produce dozens of players competing in Europe's top five leagues every season. The infrastructure to discover and develop them at scale has never been built.

The Infrastructure Gap Is the Opportunity

European and American scouts regularly miss African talent not because the talent doesn't exist — but because the data infrastructure doesn't. Verified profiles, structured evaluation, accessible clip libraries — none of these exist at scale. GUNDUA SPORTS is that infrastructure layer.

The Problem We Solve

THE GAP
IN THE MARKET

The Problem Today
BROKEN
No verified profiles. African athletes have no credible, standardised way to present themselves to international scouts and clubs.
Exploitative intermediaries. Fake agents, scam trials and predatory contracts are endemic — athletes and parents have no framework to evaluate what's legitimate.
No education infrastructure. Coaches and scouts across Africa have no access to internationally standardised professional development.
Fragmented discovery. Scouts from Europe and the USA have no systematic way to discover, evaluate and track African talent without expensive on-the-ground presence.
Zero pan-continental platform. No platform exists serving all 54 African nations with a unified discovery and development infrastructure.
What GUNDUA Builds
FIXED
Verified athlete profiles. Every profile is evaluated by a FIFA-agent-qualified scouting team and standardised for international consumption.
Education that protects. GUNDUA Academy covers agents, contracts and player rights — making athletes and parents informed, protected participants in the system.
A.F.C.A.S. certification. Premier League-pedigreed coaching and scouting education reaching African professionals for the first time through a structured credential framework.
International partner networks. Direct connections to FutbolJobs (Spain, 60+ countries) and Soccer Syndicate (USA) — verified placement pathways for the athletes we evaluate.
Pan-African infrastructure. A unified platform covering all 54 nations, distributed through a CSE (Certified Sports Educator) network that scales without proportional cost.
Revenue Architecture

SIX STREAMS.
ONE PLATFORM.

GUNDUA SPORTS is designed to monetise every layer of the talent ecosystem — from free entry points to premium institutional partnerships. Multiple revenue streams reduce dependency and create compounding value.

01
Primary
Events Revenue

Athlete registration fees, scout and club attendance packages, sponsorship, broadcast rights. Each GUNDUA Showcase generates direct revenue from multiple participant categories simultaneously.

High-margin · scalable format · multi-stakeholder
02
Primary
Academy Subscriptions

Tiered access to GUNDUA Academy — free foundation, AFCAS-certified courses at subsidised pricing, institutional licences for clubs and federations. Recurring SaaS-model revenue.

Recurring · low churn · institutional ceiling
03
Primary
Placement Fees

Commission on successful player placements — when a GUNDUA-verified athlete signs a contract with a partner club or academy, GUNDUA earns a placement fee commensurate with contract value.

Performance-based · high-value per transaction
04
Secondary
Club Recruitment Access

Subscription access for clubs and academies to the GUNDUA verified athlete database — filtered by position, region, age group, evaluation score. Direct pipeline to pre-evaluated African talent.

B2B · recurring · database grows in value
05
Secondary
CSE Revenue Share

Certified Sports Educators across Africa earn revenue share on registrations, placements and course sales they generate. Scales distribution without scaling payroll — low-cost growth infrastructure.

Distributed · scalable · low fixed cost
06
Scalable
Brand & Sponsorship

Sponsorship packages across events, digital platform, and the CSE programme. As GUNDUA SPORTS grows its athlete and educator database, it becomes an increasingly valuable media and marketing platform for sport brands targeting Africa.

High-growth ceiling · brand equity multiplier
