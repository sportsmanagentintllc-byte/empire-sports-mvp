Our Story

WHERE WE

The Problem We Saw
The Best Talent in the World Goes Undiscovered

Africa produces extraordinary athletes. Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Kenya, Ethiopia — the continent is a generator of world-class sporting talent that Europe and America have been drawing from for decades.

But the infrastructure to systematically discover, evaluate and develop that talent has never been built. Scouts travel blind. Athletes submit to fake agents. Parents sign contracts they don't understand. Coaches have no pathway to international credentials.

The talent exists. The system to surface it does not. That is the gap GUNDUA SPORTS was built to fill.

What We Built
The Infrastructure Africa's Sports Ecosystem Needed

GUNDUA SPORTS is built by people who understand this industry from the inside — the regulations, the exploitation, the gaps, and the opportunity. Founded by a FIFA agent exam-qualified professional with deep roots in the African football world.

We have established relationships with A.F.C.A.S. (UK) — Premier League-pedigreed coaching and scouting educators — FutbolJobs (Spain), one of Europe's leading football recruitment platforms across 60+ countries, and Soccer Syndicate (USA), giving our athletes direct visibility into the American market.

GUNDUA is not a concept. It is live, operational, and growing. Africa's talent deserves a platform built to its scale.
