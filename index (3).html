Event Format

HOW EVERY
GUNDUA EVENT
IS STRUCTURED

Every GUNDUA event runs in two phases — education first, then exposure. Athletes leave with both a credential and a record on the international stage.

01
Phase One
COACHING CLINIC
3 days · Intensive
Coaching seminars led by A.F.C.A.S. (UK) certified educators
Player development sessions using Soccer Syndicate USA framework
Tactical workshops and game analysis
Certificates of participation issued to coaches and athletes
Sports psychology and mental performance modules
View Academy Curriculum
02
Phase Two
TRIALS & SHOWCASE
7 days · Competitive
U17 and U19 competitive tournament — tryout teams and invited squads
International scouts in attendance from UK, Spain, USA and beyond
Athletes evaluated against verified GUNDUA scouting criteria
Written scouting reports issued to all participating athletes
Top performers connected directly to partner clubs and academies

Who Attends

BUILT FOR
EVERYONE
IN THE ROOM

01
Athletes

U17 and U19 players competing for evaluated profiles and direct exposure to international scouts and clubs. Both male and female.

02
Coaches

Grassroots and professional coaches attending the clinic phase for AFCAS-backed CPD and certificates of participation.

03
International Scouts

Scouts from UK, Spain, USA and global networks attending to evaluate talent against GUNDUA's verified criteria.

04
Clubs & Academies

Club representatives and academy directors attending to identify talent for their programmes. Pre-registration required.

05
Educators

Sports psychologists, fitness coaches, nutritionists and sports scientists contributing to the clinic programme.

06
Parents & Guardians

Parents of attending athletes receive orientation on the process, their child's evaluation, and next steps. Transparency is core to everything we do.
